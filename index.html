📋 Key Findings & Recommendations

Invest in School Sports

Peak participation is ages 5-14. School-based entry points convert 30-46% to club membership.

Close Gender Gaps

Female AFL participation trails male by 9-10 points across all ages. Targeted outreach needed.

Optimise Funding ROI

Swimming and Cycling deliver 3.8 and 2.8 medals per $10M. Basketball achieves only 1.5.
