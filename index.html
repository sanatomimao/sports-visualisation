📋 Key Findings & Recommendations

Youth Participation is Critical

Peak participation occurs at ages 5-14. Invest in school sports programs.

Significant Gender Gaps

Increase female participation in AFL and Soccer through targeted programs.

Optimise Funding Efficiency

Basketball and Tennis show lower ROI. Review funding allocation.
